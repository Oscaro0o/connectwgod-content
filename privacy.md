# Privacy Policy — ConnectWGod

Last updated: June 29, 2026

## Overview

ConnectWGod is a widget app that displays quotes from the Bible, Torah, and Quran. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

ConnectWGod does not:
- Collect or store any personal information
- Require account creation or login
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- Share any data with third parties

## Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Your preferences (selected sources, display settings, notification time)
- Your premium unlock status

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

## Remote Content

The app may fetch quote content from GitHub (github.com) to update its database. No personal information is sent during this process.

## In-App Purchases

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not process or store any payment information.
